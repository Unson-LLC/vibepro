# VibePro Operating Philosophy

VibePro is designed around a few operating rules:

- Intent comes before implementation.
- Evidence is more useful when it is current, structured, and attached to the change.
- Optional code-structure lenses help decide what to inspect, but they do not prove correctness.
- PR bodies should stay concise; detailed evidence belongs in artifacts.
- Missing optional integrations must not make ordinary PR work impossible.

The goal is not to remove engineering judgment. The goal is to give that judgment a better working surface.
